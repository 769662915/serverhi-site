## Key Takeaways for Docker Production Deployment
1. **Supply chain vulnerabilities move fast** — from disclosure to active exploitation in 2 days (Splunk case)
2. **Container-adjacent services** (NGINX proxies, logging agents, SD-WAN) are primary attack vectors
3. **Auto-update strategies in Docker Compose** are critical for rapid patch deployment
4. **Health checks and restart policies** must be combined with version-pinned images for safe rollouts
5. **Network segmentation** between containers prevents lateral movement when a single container is compromised
